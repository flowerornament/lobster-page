How it works

Built for speed.
Built to delegate.

Lobster follows a simple rule: respond fast, then figure out who's best suited to handle the work.

Step 01 📬

Message arrives

Lobster monitors Telegram 24/7. The moment a message hits your inbox, it's received in milliseconds.

Step 02 ⚡

Quick tasks: handled

Simple questions, reminders, lookups — Lobster handles these directly and responds within seconds.

Step 03 🤖

Heavy tasks: delegated

Anything that takes real effort gets handed off to a specialized subagent so Lobster stays responsive.

Step 04 ✅

Results delivered

When the subagent finishes, Lobster relays the results back to you — clean, concise, mobile-friendly.
